Starsplosion

Launch and smash your way through an onslaught of celestial objects, collect power ups, and amass spacedust in your journey through the cosmos. This arcade-style game blends classic pinball and shoot 'em up mechanics, and requires quick reflexes and careful precision in order to score big. This project is currently under development, and is slated to release for Android, iOS, and all major operating systems in 2020. It will include a variety of themes, game modes, and challenge levels to choose from.


Ghost Town

Ghost Town is a puzzle-adventure game about an individual who awakens one day as a ghost in a myserious forest of the underworld. Having forgotten nearly all memory of life in the world of the living, this individual must seek the assistance of a colourful cast of characters that inhabit this realm if they are to procure any chance of escape. The first act of the game is available for Windows, macOS, and Linux on itch.io. It is also fully playable on original Game Boy hardware, and is designed with the limitations of the console in mind.
