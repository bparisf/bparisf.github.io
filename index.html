Hello, world. My name is Paris Fleming and thanks for checking out my landing page. A bit about myself: I like to make games and music, and I am generally interested in ventures that allow me to combine my interests in computer science and the arts. My formal background is in linguistics, and consequently I am also interested in many of the emerging technologies that involve natural language processing.


This site is primarily home to the games that I have released or am currently developing. Though I am a developer-by-hobby, I have long tinkered with making games, and have tried my hand at various software and modes of development. You can get a taste for some of my recent projects by heading over to the projects section of the site. Besides this site, information and downloads for some of my games can also be found over at itch.io. I would love for you to check them out!


I can also be found over on twitter, where I mostly post updates about my current projects, as well as others from around the web that interest me. Feel free to reach out and say hi.
